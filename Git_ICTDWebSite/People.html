Faculty

Shameem Ahmed

I am the director of the ICTD (ICT for Development) Research Lab, an inter-disciplinary research group at Western Washington University. I am also a co-director of the Western Human Computer Interaction (Western HCI) research group. My research interest lies in the intersection of the following areas: HCI, ICTD, mHealth, and Mobile Computing. I collaborate with researchers from several disciplines (e.g.,Computer Science, Information Science, Business and Management, Social Science, Library Science, and Medicine) who came from all over the world (e.g., University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Cornell University, University of Melbourne in Australia, Dhaka University in Bangladesh, and North South University in Bangladesh).
