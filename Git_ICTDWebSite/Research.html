Current Research Projects

Mobile application for smoking cessation
Investigating the literature on smoking cessation and surveying the existing technologies that aid in this effort. We are developing a gamified, buddy-system based mobile application which supports a competitive environment for "cessation buddies" to kick the habit.

mHealth Literacy
With so many mobile health application on the application marketplace, it is increasingly difficult to determine the appropriate tools for the appropriate users. Digital literacy, and health literacy vary greatly amongst the general public, and to alleviate the issues of finding the right mHealth tool, we are developing an application which will assess a users level of health and technology literacy, and use that knowledge to identify the best mHealth applications for their needs and abilities

Technology to support Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
Currently conducting research into existing technologies in support of individuals with ASD.
